Privacy Policy - FundHive

FundHive is a minimal Android expense tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This app does not collect or transmit any personal information outside your device.

Data We Store

FundHive stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Expense amount
  • Expense category/type

How We Use Your Data

The stored data is used solely to calculate totals, display expenses in a list, and provide you with an overview of your spending.

Sharing & Third Parties

FundHive does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. All data remains on your device.
